About OBMC

O.B. Montessori Center follows the Montessori method of Dr. Maria Montessori. Students are guided to become independent, confident, and responsible. The school offers programs from Casa to Senior High School, with prepared classrooms, respectful routines, and helpful teachers.

At OBMC, students learn not only academics but also life skills like time management, leadership, and cooperation. The campus in Angeles is located along MacArthur Highway, near different local communities in Pampanga.

Programs at OBMC Angeles

Casa

Early childhood program where young children use real materials and learn through play and exploration.

Grade School & JHS

Elementary and Junior High levels that develop strong foundations in Math, Science, English, Filipino, and values.

Senior High School

Tracks and strands that prepare students for college and future careers with research, projects, and real-life tasks.
