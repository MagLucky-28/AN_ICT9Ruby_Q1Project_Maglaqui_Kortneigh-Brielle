Courses Offered: Casa to SHS

Casa (Preschool)

Casa is OBMCâ€™s preschool level for young learners. Children use practical life, sensorial, language, and math materials. The environment is colorful and child-sized so they can move, explore, and learn at their own pace.

In this stage, students start building confidence, independence, and love for learning.

Grade School and Junior High School

Grade School covers Grades 1 to 6, while Junior High School covers Grades 7 to 10. Subjects include English, Filipino, Math, Science, Araling Panlipunan, and other enrichment areas.

Students practice research, group work, and presentations. Classrooms are designed to keep lessons engaging and interactive.

Senior High School (SHS)

OBMC Senior High School prepares students for college and future careers. Depending on the campus, possible strands include STEM, ABM, HUMSS, and other academic tracks.

Students work on research papers, performance tasks, and real-life projects while still following the Montessori values of order, respect, and responsibility.
